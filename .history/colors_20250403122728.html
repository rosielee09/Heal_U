About This Styleguide

This document provides guidelines for maintaining visual consistency across our product.

This styleguide serves as the single source of truth for our design language. It contains all the elements needed to create cohesive user interfaces, including colors, typography, logo usage, and graphic elements.

Use the navigation on the left to explore different sections of the styleguide.

How to Use This Styleguide

This styleguide is organized into the following sections:

  • Colors: Our color palette, including primary, secondary, and neutral colors.
  • Typography: Font families, sizes, weights, and line heights.
  • Logo: Guidelines for logo usage, clear space, and variations.
  • Graphics: Icons, illustrations, and other graphic elements.
  • References: External resources and documentation.
