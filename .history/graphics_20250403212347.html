Graphics

This document provides guidelines for maintaining visual consistency across our product.

Menu bar

Home

The Home button navigates users to the home screen, providing quick access to the main interface.

Diagnose

The diagnose button navigates users to the Diagnose page, allowing them to reassess their condition anytime, especially if their pain level changes significantly.

Progress

The Progress button takes users to the Progress page, where they can track their journey from the beginning to their current pain condition.

Blog

The Blog button takes users to the blog page, allowing them to interact with others and find motivation through shared experiences.

Setting

The Settings button takes users to the settings page, where they can customize their preferences and personalize their experience.
