Logo

Welcome to our comprehensive design system

Concept

The Heal_U logo is inspired by the natural curvature of the human spine, simplified into a series of dots to create a clean and modern aesthetic. These dots are thoughtfully structured to form the shape of the letter ‘H,’ symbolizing Heal_U while reinforcing the app’s focus on health and posture. The design visually communicates balance, alignment, and well-being, embodying the core mission of promoting better posture and spinal health.

Logo Logo Type Description

Light Mode

In light mode, the logo feature darker colors or high-contrast elements to stand out against lighter backgrounds. This ensures clear visibility and legibility.

Dark Mode

In dark mode, the logo use lighter colors or white elements to maintain contrast against dark backgrounds. The goal is to maintain legibility without losing brand identity in low-light settings.

Icon + Wordmark

A logo with a wordmark combines a symbol or icon with stylized text that represents the brand name. The wordmark is typically the focal point of the design, with the logo symbol complementing it.

Logo Sizings

The logo design begins by defining the height as the base for proportions. A square is used to cover the height, establishing the boundary for the logo. Equal padding is added to each corner of the square to ensure minimal space around the logo, providing balance and clarity within the designated area. This method ensures a visually cohesive and well-proportioned design.

Logo Font

Inter


The Inter typeface was selected for the Heal_U logo due to its clean, modern aesthetics and exceptional legibility. Designed with a strong focus on clarity and usability, Inter conveys a sense of trust, simplicity, and professionalism—qualities that align closely with the values of the Heal_U brand. Its geometric yet humanist letterforms offer a harmonious balance between approachability and precision, making it an ideal choice for a wellness-focused identity. The use of Inter supports the brand’s mission to create a calm, supportive, and user-centered experience.
