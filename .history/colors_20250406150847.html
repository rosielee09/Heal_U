Our brand and interface colors

Color Palette

A comprehensive overview of our brand's color system.

Primary Color

#29424D
  • Reasoning: Represents physical recovery, making it ideal for the Heal_U app.
  • Psychological Impact: Evokes a sense of trust, stability, and renewal, essential for a health-focused application.
  • Usability Benefits: Cool, muted tone reduces visual strain, enhancing user comfort. Supports a soothing user experience, aligning with our mission to promote well-being and healing.

Secondary Color

#CFC4F4
  • Reasoning: Applied to buttons, cards, and chips to ensure consistency while evoking a sense of warmth and sophistication.
  • Usability: Provides gentle contrast against our primary navy shade, improving readability and accessibility.
  • Symbolism: Purple is associated with balance, abd mindfulness, aligning with the Heal_U appâ€™s mission of holistic recovery.

Tertiary Color

#F8F3EC
  • Reasoning: Soft and neutral tone complements the primary and secondary colors. Enhances the overall balance of the color scheme.
  • Psychological Impact: Creates a sense of warmth, comfort, and cleanliness. Associated with calmness and clarity, reinforcing a healing environment.
  • Usability Benefits: Provides a gentle background for content without overwhelming the user.

Accent Color

#CDF06B
  • Reasoning Appeal: Applied to buttons in the dark version and chips for emphasis. A vibrant, fresh shade that adds energy and contrast.
  • Psychological Impact: Green is associated with growth, renewal, and vitality, aligning with Heal_U's focus on recovery and well-being.
  • Usability Benefits: Provides strong contrast against dark mode elements for better readability and accessibility.
