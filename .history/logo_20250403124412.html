Logo

The concept of making the logo

The Heal_U logo is inspired by the natural curvature of the human spine, simplified into a series of dots to create a clean and modern aesthetic. These dots are thoughtfully structured to form the shape of the letter ‘H,’ symbolizing Heal_Uwhile reinforcing the app’s focus on health and posture. The design visually communicates balance, alignment, and well-being, embodying the core mission of promoting better posture and spinal health.
