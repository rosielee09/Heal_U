Accessibility & Color

Guidelines for ensuring our color usage meets accessibility standards.

Our color system is designed with accessibility in mind. We ensure that:

  • Text and interactive elements have sufficient color contrast (WCAG 2.1 AA compliant)
  • Color is not the only means of conveying information
  • Text on colored backgrounds maintains readability
  • We accommodate users with various types of color blindness

Contrast Ratio Examples

White text on Primary Navy (7.9:1)
White text on Primary Blue (2.9:1)
Dark text on Light Background (13.7:1)
Color Combination Contrast Ratio WCAG AA (4.5:1)
White on Navy 7.9:1 Pass
White on Blue 2.9:1 Fail
Dark on Light BG 13.7:1 Pass

For text that fails the contrast test, we either:

  1. Use larger and bolder text (minimum 18pt or 14pt bold)
  2. Choose a different color combination
  3. Add additional visual cues beyond color
